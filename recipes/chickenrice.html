Chicken Rice

Description

This delicious and hearty one-pot meal is a favourite of mine.

Recipe makes four servings.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs (800g)
  • Chicken bone broth (1000ml)
  • Onion (2 cups chopped)
  • Carrots (2 cups chopped)
  • Okra (2 cups chopped)
  • Yellow potatoes (2 cups chopped)
  • Avocado oil (2 tbsp)
  • Kosher salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • White jasmine rice
  • Frozen peas

Steps

  1. Add bone broth and chicken thighs to crockpot or instant pot (optional: brown chicken thighs in pan for 90 seconds per side).
  2. Add chopped onions, carrots, okra, potatoes to pot.
  3. Season liberally with salt.
  4. Add avocado oil.
  5. Set crockpot, instant pot, or other slow cooker device to 6-8hrs on low setting.
  6. Add frozen peas 15 minutes before slow cooker is done.
  7. Cook rice separately and combine with other slow cooker contents.
